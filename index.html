Jie Zhang

I am an Associate Professor in School of Automation and Electrical Engineering at University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB), China. My research interests lie at 3D perception with vision or radar and pattern recognition, especially focusing on static/dynamic point cloud processing, active vision reconstruction and applications to a variety of industrial scenarios. Before joining USTB, I received my PhD from Beihang University , China in 2018, advised by Prof. Junhua Sun. During 2016-2018, I was a visiting PhD student at the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh, UK under the supervision of Prof. Robert B. Fisher .

Research Overview

My research interests lie at computer vision and pattern recognition, especially focusing on 3D vision theories, algorithms and their applications to industry.
